# CybersecurityBlog-Restaurant
A blog-style webpage designed for privately owned small businesses in the hospitality industry. The goal of the blog is to educate business owners and encourage best practice.

The blog reviews common vectors of attack, such as Phishing, POS systems, Wifi, and Physical Attacks. It examines how to protect yourself against each kind of attack, and links to outside resources. The blog also contains a webpage with a cybersecurity checklist for managers/owners on the floor of a restaurant.
